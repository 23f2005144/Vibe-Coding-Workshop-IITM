# Pokémon Flappy Bird

A simple Flappy Bird clone with a Pokémon twist! Dodge Team Rocket obstacles and collect Pokémon items.

## How to Play

- **Jump**: Press <kbd>Space</kbd>, click, or tap to make Pikachu jump.
- **Goal**: Survive as long as you can, dodging Team Rocket and collecting Pokéballs and berries to raise your score!

## Setup

1. **Copy Assets:**
   - Download graphics and audio below, and place them into the `assets/` folder:
     ```
     assets/
       pikachu.png
       team-rocket.png
       pokeball.png
       berry.png
       pokemon-theme.mp3
     ```
2. **Recommended Asset Sources (no copyright):**
   - Pikachu ([Free PNG](https://www.cleanpng.com/png-pikachu-pokemon-go-pokeball-pok%C3%A9mon-x-and-y-pikachu-6384370/))
   - Team Rocket (use a balloon/rocket image or search "team rocket PNG" or use [this fan art PNG with credit](https://www.pngwing.com/en/free-png-vwhfc))
   - Pokeball ([Public Domain PNG](https://www.pngmart.com/files/22/Pokeball-PNG-Transparent.png))
   - Berry (Try [Sitrus Berry PNG](https://www.pngwing.com/en/free-png-rmwdn))
   - Music: [Pokemon Theme Chiptune/Remix](https://freemusicarchive.org/music/Rolemusic/~/pokemon-theme-chiptune-remix/) OR any free-use Pokémon remix

3. **Run the Game**
   - Open `index.html` in your browser!

---

If you want to add new Pokémon or features, just swap images in the `/assets` folder or modify `game.js`.
